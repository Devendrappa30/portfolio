Service Offerings

Comprehensive project management services tailored to your needs using Waterfall, Agile, and XP methodologies.

Waterfall Project Management

  • Structured project planning with clear phases (Requirements, Design, Implementation, Testing, Deployment).
  • Detailed Gantt charts and milestone tracking.
  • Risk management and stakeholder communication.
  • Ideal for projects with fixed requirements and regulatory compliance needs.

Agile Project Management

  • Iterative delivery with sprints and continuous feedback.
  • Facilitation of Scrum ceremonies (Daily Standups, Sprint Planning, Retrospectives).
  • Tracking KPIs like Velocity, Cycle Time, and Burndown Charts.
  • Suitable for dynamic software development projects.

Extreme Programming (XP)

  • Emphasis on technical excellence with practices like Pair Programming, TDD, and Continuous Integration.
  • Rapid delivery of small, functional increments.
  • Code quality metrics and defect management.
  • Best for high-change, developer-driven projects.

Support Project Management

  • Management of IT support and maintenance projects.
  • Incident tracking, SLA monitoring, and resolution time analysis.
  • Coordination of support teams and escalation processes.
  • Ensuring system uptime and client satisfaction.

Additional Services

  • Custom dashboard setup (Jira, Azure DevOps, Asana).
  • Process optimization and team training.
  • Stakeholder reporting and documentation (Confluence).
  • Risk and change management across methodologies.
