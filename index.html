Case Studies

Narayana Health - Zendesk Sell & Support Implementation

Client: Narayana Health (Healthcare, 250 agents)

Challenges: Manual lead handling, inefficient support, data migration gaps, lack of self-service.

Solutions: Implemented Zendesk Sell/Support, automated ticket routing, self-service portal, integrated HMS/EMR/Telephony, 25+ custom reports.

Results: 30% increase in lead conversions, 40% faster response times, 25% improved resolution speed, 35% better SLA compliance, 40% reduction in manual effort.

Technologies: Zendesk Sell/Support, HMS, EMR, Telephony, Agile methodology.

Glassco - Zendesk Sell CRM Implementation

Client: Glassco Laboratory Equipment (1000+ employees, 25 sales agents)

Challenges: Manual sales tracking, limited lead management, expense calculation issues, lack of integrations.

Solutions: Configured Zendesk Sell, integrated Google Maps/SAP/LinkedIn, multi-channel communication (WhatsApp, Facebook), automated expense management.

Results: Improved sales tracking, higher lead conversions, automated expense management, seamless integrations.
